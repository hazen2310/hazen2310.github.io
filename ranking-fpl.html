About FPL ⬡

What is the FPL?

FPL is an in-house league system where top players and selected new talents compete in private solo-queue matchmaking to train, network and stream all-star content.

When is FPL?

FPL seasons run continuously with structured scheduling to ensure maximum participation from pro players.

How do I qualify?

Players can qualify by reaching the top of the FPL Challenger rankings or through direct invitations based on professional performance.
